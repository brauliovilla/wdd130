Ecuador

Ecuador is located right on the equatorial line, between two countries: Colombia and Peru. Its capital, Quito, is a city brimming with biodiversity and boasts some of the world's most visited tourist attractions, such as the Middle of the World, located north of Quito. Ecuador also boasts beautiful volcanoes to visit, some of the largest in the world.
